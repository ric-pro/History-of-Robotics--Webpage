Hiroshi Ishiguro

Field Robotics, Android Science
Birthdate February 2, 1963
Contributions Hiroshi Ishiguro is known for his work in humanoid robotics and android science. He has developed numerous lifelike robots, including the Geminoid series, which are designed to resemble and behave like humans. His research focuses on understanding human behavior and consciousness through robotics.

Rodney Brooks

Field Artificial Intelligence, Robotics
Birthdate December 30, 1954
Contributions Rodney Brooks is a pioneering figure in the field of robotics and artificial intelligence. He co-founded iRobot, a company that developed the Roomba vacuum cleaner and military robots. Brooks also contributed to the development of autonomous robots with his work on behavior-based robotics, emphasizing the importance of simple reactive behaviors in complex systems.

Cynthia Breazeal

Field Human-Robot Interaction, Social Robotics
Birthdate November 15, 1967
Contributions Cynthia Breazeal is a renowned roboticist known for her work in human-robot interaction and social robotics. She developed Kismet, one of the first social robots capable of expressing emotions and engaging in social interactions with humans. Breazeal's research focuses on creating robots that can understand and respond to human emotions, with applications in therapy, education, and entertainment.

Sebastian Thrun

Field IRobotics, Artificial Intelligence
Birthdate May 14, 1967
Contributions Sebastian Thrun is a computer scientist and entrepreneur known for his contributions to robotics and artificial intelligence. He led the development of Google's self-driving car project (now known as Waymo) and co-founded Udacity, an online education platform. Thrun's research spans various areas, including autonomous navigation, machine learning, and robotics education.

Helen Greiner

Field Robotics, Entrepreneurship
Birthdate December 6, 1967
Contributions Helen Greiner is a robotics entrepreneur and co-founder of iRobot, where she played a key role in the development of consumer and military robots. She led the creation of the Roomba vacuum cleaner and the PackBot military robot, which has been used for bomb disposal and reconnaissance. Greiner is a prominent figure in the robotics industry, advocating for the widespread adoption of robots in everyday life.
